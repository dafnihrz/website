Nike’s “Sun Club” Air Force 1 Is Dipped in Pastel Hues

Nike has unveiled a new multicolored Air Force 1 that furthers the brand’s “Sun Club” series.


Featuring a color-blocked construction, the eye-catching offering is dipped in pastel hues like sun gold, hot curry, baby blue, lavender and salmon. But what brings these vivid shades to life is the mix of recycled woven and canvas fabrics that comprise the shoe’s upper. The sole is dipped in light gray, while the midsole comes in crisp white.


Additionally, the heels feature embroidered Nike “Sun Club” logos in black and yellow. Finishing off the Air Force 1’s is a two-toned Swoosh in lavender and tangerine.
